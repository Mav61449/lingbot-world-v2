## 🚀 Getting Started

Once you install the app, click the desktop icon to launch your first session. The main screen shows a simple dashboard. You see a menu that allows you to name your new world.

Select "Create New" to begin. The app prompts you for a starting theme. Choose from the list or type your own description. The system interprets your text to build the logic for the characters within your world. 

## 🎮 Interacting with Your World

After the world loads, a chat box appears at the bottom of your screen. Type your messages here. Press the "Enter" key to send them to the residents of your world. 

Characters respond based on their personality profiles. You can change these profiles in the settings menu. You can move between different areas of your world by clicking the map icon in the top left corner. Each area contains unique objects and characters.

## 🛠️ Customizing Settings

You can adjust how the app performs via the settings menu. Open this by clicking the gear icon.

*   Language: You can change the base language here.
*   Interaction Speed: This controls how fast characters write their responses.
*   Auto-Save: Keep this option on to ensure your world state remains safe.
*   Theme: Switch between light and dark modes to match your preference.

## 🧩 Saving and Loading

The software auto-saves your progress whenever you interact with a character. To save manually, click the disk icon. To reopen a previous world, return to the main dashboard. Click "Load World" and select your file from the list.

## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

*   **Can I share my worlds?**
    Yes. You can export your save files and send them to friends. They can load these files into their own copy of the app.

*   **Does this require a constant internet connection?**
    Only the first time you run the app. After the initial setup, you can use the software while offline. 

*   **Is my data private?**
    Your worlds reside entirely on your local hard drive. The developers do not collect or view your custom content.

*   **What if the app freezes?**
    Close the application and open it again. The auto-save feature ensures you lose very little progress.

*   **Are there limits to the size of my world?**
    You can build as much as your hard drive allows. Keep in mind that extremely large worlds may take longer to load.

## 📖 Best Practices for Building

*   Keep descriptions specific so the system generates clear imagery.
*   Assign clear roles to your characters to prevent confusion during conversations.
*   Test your world often to ensure the interactions flow as you expect.
*   Use the search function if you lose track of items in your environment.
